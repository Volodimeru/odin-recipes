Homemade BBQ Sauce

Description

This recipe has a lot of sweetness and spice, so if you're looking for a classic homemade BBQ sauce that's easy to make, look no further.

Ingredients

  • Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, vinegar, water, and Worcestershire sauce in a blender.
  3. Season with mustard, paprika, salt, pepper, and hot pepper sauce. Blend until smooth.
  4. Enjoy!
